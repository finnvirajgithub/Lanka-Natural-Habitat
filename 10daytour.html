10 - Day Tour

10-Day Sri Lanka Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Colombo Arrival

Arrival at Colombo airport. Transfer to Habarana resort for check-in and leisure.

Dinner & Overnight: Habarana Resort

Day 2: Sigiriya Rock Fortress & Village Visit

After breakfast, visit the Sigiriya Rock Fortress, then enjoy a canoe and bullock cart ride. Experience a local cooking demonstration and lunch at a village.

Dinner & Overnight: Habarana Resort

Day 3: Minneriya Jeep Safari

Explore Minneriya National Park by Jeep, where you can spot elephants and other wildlife.

Dinner & Overnight: Habarana Resort

Day 4: Spice Garden Visit & Kandy

Travel to Kandy, stopping at Matale Spice Garden for lunch and a tour.

Dinner & Overnight: Kandy Hotel

Day 5: Temple of the Tooth & Dance Show

Visit the famous Temple of the Tooth Relic and enjoy a traditional dance performance in the evening.

Dinner & Overnight: Kandy Hotel

Day 6: Scenic Train to Nuwara Eliya

Take a scenic train from Kandy to Nanu Oya, then transfer to your hotel in Nuwara Eliya.

Dinner & Overnight: Nuwara Eliya Hotel

Day 7: Nuwara Eliya Tour & Transfer to Beruwala

Explore Nuwara Eliya, including tea plantations. Then transfer to Beruwala beach for relaxation.

Dinner & Overnight: Beruwala Resort

Day 8: Turtle Hatchery & Madu River Safari

Option to visit the Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery or relax at the beach. Enjoy a Madu River boat safari.

Dinner & Overnight: Beruwala Resort

Day 9: Galle Fort or Relax at the Beach

Explore Galle Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, or continue relaxing at the resort.

Dinner & Overnight: Beruwala Resort

Day 10: Departure

Transfer to Colombo airport for your departure.

End of Tour
