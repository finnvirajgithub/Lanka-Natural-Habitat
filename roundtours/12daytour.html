12 - Day Tour

12-Day Sri Lanka Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival / Relax in Negombo

Arrive in Sri Lanka and transfer to your hotel in Negombo. Relax on the beach or by the pool. Enjoy a sunset stroll along the shore.

Overnight stay: Seafront hotel in Negombo.

Day 2: Elephant Sanctuary / Village Tour / Sigiriya Fortress

Visit Pinnawala Elephant Sanctuary, enjoy a village tour, and explore Sigiriya Rock Fortress at sunset.

Overnight stay: Eco-friendly resort in Sigiriya.

Day 3: Polonnaruwa / Elephant Jeep Safari

Explore ancient Polonnaruwa by bicycle, then head to Minneriya National Park for an elephant safari.

Overnight stay: Nature-friendly resort in Sigiriya.

Day 4: Cave Temple / Kandy Cultural Show

Visit the Dambulla Cave Temple, then proceed to Kandy to visit the Temple of the Tooth Relic and enjoy a cultural dance show.

Overnight stay: Mount View Hotel in Kandy.

Day 5: Kitulgala Rafting Adventure

Experience thrilling white water rafting in Kitulgala or relax in your hotel surrounded by nature.

Overnight stay: Mount View Hotel in Kitulgala.

Day 6: Nuwara Eliya Tea Plantations

Journey through tea plantations and waterfalls to Nuwara Eliya. Explore British colonial buildings and enjoy a tea factory tour.

Overnight stay: Villa in Nuwara Eliya.

Day 7: Train Journey to Ella / Mini Adam's Peak

Take a scenic train ride to Ella. Visit the Nine Arch Bridge, Ravana Waterfall, and trek to Mini Adam’s Peak.

Overnight stay: Mount View Hotel in Ella.

Day 8: Yala National Park Safari

Enjoy a 4x4 safari in Yala National Park, where you might spot leopards, sloth bears, and elephants.

Overnight stay: Safari resort in Yala.

Day 9: Leisure Day on the Beach in Mirissa

Relax on the beautiful Mirissa beach. Optional activities include jet skiing, snorkeling, and diving.

Overnight stay: Seafront resort in Mirissa.

Day 10: Whale Watching / Beach Day

Go whale watching in the morning, then spend the rest of the day relaxing on the beach.

Overnight stay: Seafront resort in Mirissa.

Day 11: Galle Fort / Turtle Hatchery

Visit Galle Fort, take a boat ride in Madu River, and visit a turtle hatchery in the afternoon.

Overnight stay: Beach resort in Bentota.

Day 12: Colombo City Tour / Departure

Explore Colombo city, visit landmarks like the Red Mosque and Gangarama Temple, and enjoy your final meal before your departure.

Drop-off: Colombo Airport or your hotel.
