14 - Day Tour

14-Day Sri Lanka Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Sri Lanka / Welcome!

Welcome to Sri Lanka! Arrival at Colombo CMB airport, transfer to hotel in Negombo. Relax by the beach.

Overnight Stay: Beachfront hotel in Negombo

Day 2: Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage / Sigiriya Rock Fortress

Visit Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage and Sigiriya Rock Fortress.

Overnight Stay: Resort in Dambulla/Sigiriya

Day 3: Cultural Village Tour / Pidurangala Rock

Experience a traditional village tour, optional Pidurangala hike.

Overnight Stay: Resort in Sigiriya

Days 4-5: Relax on Pasikudah Beach

Head to the beautiful Pasikudah beach for two days of relaxation and optional water activities.

Overnight Stay: Resort in Pasikudah

Day 6: Polonnaruwa Ancient City / Minneriya Safari

Explore Polonnaruwa ancient city by bicycle, then go on an elephant safari at Minneriya National Park.

Overnight Stay: Resort in Dambulla

Day 7: Kandy / Temple of the Tooth

Visit the Dambulla Golden Temple en route to Kandy. Explore Temple of the Tooth in the evening.

Overnight Stay: Mountain view hotel in Kandy

Day 8: Peradeniya Botanical Gardens / Kandy City Tour

Visit Peradeniya Botanical Gardens and explore the city of Kandy. Attend a cultural dance performance.

Overnight Stay: Hotel in Kandy

Day 9: Nuwara Eliya / Tea Plantations

Travel to Nuwara Eliya, visit tea plantations, and explore the town known as "Little England."

Overnight Stay: Hotel in Nuwara Eliya

Day 10: Horton Plains National Park / World's End

Enjoy a hike through Horton Plains National Park and witness the stunning view at World's End.

Overnight Stay: Hotel in Nuwara Eliya

Day 11: Ella / Nine Arches Bridge

Visit the scenic town of Ella, hike up Little Adam's Peak, and explore the iconic Nine Arches Bridge.

Overnight Stay: Hotel in Ella

Day 12: Yala National Park Safari

Go on a thrilling wildlife safari in Yala National Park, home to leopards, elephants, and more.

Overnight Stay: Safari lodge near Yala

Day 13: Galle Fort / Beach Relaxation

Visit the historic Galle Fort, then relax on the beaches of the southern coast.

Overnight Stay: Beach resort in Galle

Day 14: Departure / Farewell

Transfer to Colombo for your departure. Say farewell to Sri Lanka!
