8 - Day Tour

8-Day Sri Lanka Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Airport or Hotel Pick-up / Welcome to Sri Lanka

Ayubowan! Welcome to Sri Lanka, the land of ancient history, lush green tea estates, and stunning beaches.

Pick-up from Colombo CMB Airport or any hotel in Colombo/Negombo, followed by a transfer to your hotel in Negombo. Relax by the beach or pool, and take a sunset walk along the seashore.

Overnight Stay:

Seafront hotel in Negombo.

Day 2: Elephant Feeding / Cultural Village / Sigiriya

Begin your journey to Dambulla after breakfast. Visit the Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage and enjoy a village tour with tuk-tuk rides and authentic Sri Lankan food. Visit the iconic Sigiriya Lion Rock Fortress for sunset views.

Overnight Stay:

Nature-friendly resort in Dambulla.

Day 3: Cave Temple / Kandy Tour / Cultural Show

After breakfast, head to Kandy and visit the Dambulla Cave Temple. Explore the Temple of the Tooth in Kandy, and enjoy a cultural dance show in the evening.

Overnight Stay:

Mount-view hotel in Kandy.

Day 4: Tea Plantations / Nuwara Eliya

Travel through lush tea plantations to Nuwara Eliya. Visit a tea factory and enjoy the charming colonial architecture of the town.

Overnight Stay:

Hotel in Nuwara Eliya.

Day 5: Train Journey to Ella / Nature Tour

Experience a scenic train ride to Ella, visiting the Ravana Waterfall and Nine Arch Bridge. Continue to Yala National Park for a safari adventure.

Overnight Stay:

Safari resort in Yala.

Day 6: Yala Safari / Beach Relaxation

Start with an early morning safari at Yala National Park, then relax at Mirissa beach with optional water activities like snorkeling and jet skiing.

Overnight Stay:

Beach resort in Mirissa.

Day 7: Whale Watching / Galle Tour / Turtle Hatchery

Enjoy whale watching in the morning (seasonal). Explore Galle Fort and visit a turtle hatchery, followed by a boat ride through the Madu River.

Overnight Stay:

Beach resort in Bentota.

Day 8: Colombo Tour / Airport Transfer

Explore Colombo, visiting highlights such as the Red Mosque and Gangarama Temple. Transfer to the airport for departure or return to your hotel.
