Sigiriya Day Tour

Colombo to Sigiriya Day Tour Itinerary

Departure from Colombo

05:00 AM: Early morning departure from Colombo to Sigiriya (Approx. 4-4.5 hour drive).

Stop 1: Sigiriya Lion Rock

09:30 AM – 11:00 AM: Climb the iconic Sigiriya Lion Rock.

Explore the ancient rock fortress, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Enjoy stunning panoramic views from the top, discover the famous frescoes, and stroll through the beautifully landscaped water gardens.

Lunch Break

11:30 AM – 12:30 PM: Enjoy a traditional Sri Lankan lunch at a local restaurant.

Stop 2: Traditional Village Tour

12:30 PM – 01:30 PM: Experience the local culture with a traditional village tour.

Take a bullock cart ride, learn about Sri Lankan rural life, and enjoy activities like cooking demonstrations or canoeing through the village waterways.

Stop 3: Dambulla Cave Temple

02:00 PM – 03:00 PM: Visit the famous Dambulla Cave Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Explore the ancient caves filled with Buddhist statues and murals dating back over 2,000 years.

Stop 4: National Park Safari (Minneriya, Kaudulla, or Eco Park)

03:30 PM – 05:30 PM: Go on a wildlife safari at Minneriya, Kaudulla, or Eco Park (depending on the season).

Your guide will help pick the right park for optimal wildlife sightings, including the famous "Elephant Gathering" and other animals like birds, monkeys, and crocodiles.

Return to Colombo

05:30 PM: Begin the return journey to Colombo (Approx. 4.5-hour drive).

Arrival in Colombo

10:00 PM: Arrive back in Colombo.
