Negombo City Tour

Negombo City Tour Itinerary

Stop 1: Negombo Beach

Start your tour by relaxing at the famous Negombo Beach. Enjoy the golden sands, warm waters, and take in the view of fishermen bringing in their morning catch. This is the perfect spot to unwind and capture scenic photos of the Indian Ocean.

Stop 2: Negombo Lagoon Boat Tour

Next, embark on a boat tour of the Negombo Lagoon. Glide through the lagoon’s calm waters and witness local wildlife, mangroves, and fishing communities. The lagoon is rich with marine life and is an important part of Negombo’s history as a fishing hub.

Stop 3: Negombo Dutch Fort

Visit the historic Negombo Dutch Fort, built in the 17th century. While only part of the original structure remains, the fort is a reminder of Negombo's colonial past. The fort’s surroundings are a testament to the influence of Dutch rule in the region.

Stop 4: St. Mary’s Church

Visit St. Mary’s Church, one of the largest churches in Sri Lanka. Admire the impressive architecture, frescoes, and statues that represent the deep-rooted Catholic heritage of Negombo.

Stop 5: St. Sebastian’s Church

Visit St. Sebastian’s Church, known for its gothic architecture and towering spires. This is another important Catholic landmark in the city and is a must-see for visitors interested in Sri Lanka's religious history.

Stop 6: Angurukaramulla Temple

Explore the ancient Angurukaramulla Temple, famous for its giant seated Buddha statue and colorful murals. The temple offers insight into the local Buddhist traditions and is a peaceful spot to take a break from the bustling city.

Stop 7: Negombo Fish Market

Conclude your tour with a visit to the lively Negombo Fish Market. Watch fishermen sell their fresh catch of the day, from prawns to crabs and various fish species. It’s a great opportunity to witness the vibrant seafood trade that has supported the local economy for generations.
