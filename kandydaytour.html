Kandy Day Tour

Kandy Day Tour Itinerary from Colombo

Departure from Colombo

06:00 AM:

Depart from Colombo to Pinnawala (Approx. 2.5-hour drive).

Stop 1: Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage

08:30 AM – 10:00 AM:

Visit Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage. Watch elephants bathing in the river and feeding time. Great spot for photos and learning about elephant care.

Drive to Kandy

10:00 AM – 11:30 AM:

Drive to Kandy (Approx. 1.5-hour drive).

Stop 2: Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic (Sri Dalada Maligawa)

11:30 AM – 12:30 PM:

Visit the famous Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic. Explore the rich history and cultural significance of this UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Lunch

12:30 PM – 01:30 PM:

Lunch at a local restaurant in Kandy (Recommendations: Kandy House or Balaji Dosai for a quick bite).

Stop 3: Gem Museum

01:30 PM – 02:30 PM:

Explore the Gem Museum in Kandy. Learn about Sri Lanka’s precious gems, from sapphires to rubies.

Stop 4: Wood Carvings & Silk Factory

02:30 PM – 04:00 PM:

Visit a wood carving workshop and a silk factory. Observe traditional artisans at work and shop for souvenirs.

Return to Colombo

04:00 PM:

Begin the return journey to Colombo (Approx. 3-3.5-hour drive).

Arrival in Colombo

07:30 PM:

Arrive back in Colombo.
