Udawalawe

Udawalawe is one of Sri Lanka’s premier wildlife destinations, known for its stunning landscapes and abundant wildlife. The Udawalawe National Park is the most famous attraction here, offering visitors an incredible opportunity to observe wildlife in their natural habitat, especially the iconic Sri Lankan elephant.

Key Attractions

  • Udawalawe National Park: This park is renowned for its large elephant population, and visitors are almost guaranteed to see herds of these majestic creatures. In addition to elephants, the park is home to water buffalo, sambar deer, crocodiles, and a variety of bird species, making it a paradise for wildlife enthusiasts.
  • Udawalawe Elephant Transit Home: Located near the national park, this facility rescues orphaned elephants and cares for them until they are ready to be released back into the wild. Visitors can watch the feeding of baby elephants, an experience that’s both heartwarming and educational.
  • Sri Lankan Wildlife Safaris: Udawalawe is one of the best places in Sri Lanka to enjoy a wildlife safari. The park offers jeep safaris that take visitors deep into the wilderness, allowing them to observe animals up close while enjoying the breathtaking scenery.
  • Birdwatching: The national park is also a haven for birdwatchers, with species like the grey-headed fish eagle, serpent eagle, and various water birds commonly seen around the Udawalawe reservoir. The mix of wetland and forest ecosystems attracts both resident and migratory birds.
  • Udawalawe Reservoir: A large water body located within the park, the Udawalawe Reservoir is an excellent spot to witness wildlife coming to drink. The surrounding scenery adds to the beauty of this peaceful area, with picturesque views of the water and distant mountains.

Nature and Wildlife

Udawalawe is celebrated for its diverse range of wildlife. While elephants are the primary attraction, the park also supports other large mammals like leopards, sloth bears, and spotted deer, though they are more elusive. The park’s natural beauty is enhanced by its open grasslands, forests, and wetlands, which create a variety of ecosystems that support its rich biodiversity.

Whether you're embarking on a safari or visiting the Elephant Transit Home, Udawalawe provides an unforgettable experience for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts. Its scenic landscapes and opportunities for close encounters with wildlife make it a must-visit destination for anyone exploring Sri Lanka’s natural wonders.
