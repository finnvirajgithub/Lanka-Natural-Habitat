14 - Day Tour

14-Day Sri Lanka Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Sri Lanka / Welcome!

Welcome to Sri Lanka! Upon arrival at Colombo CMB airport, meet your tour leader and transfer to your hotel in Negombo, famous for its sandy beaches and lively atmosphere.

Day Plan: Free day to relax by the beach or pool. Take a stroll along the seashore to enjoy the sunset.

Overnight Stay: Beachfront hotel in Negombo

Day 2: Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage / Sigiriya Rock Fortress

After breakfast, head to Dambulla with a stop at Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage. Feed and bathe the elephants, then continue to the UNESCO-listed Sigiriya Rock Fortress for a sunset visit.

Day Plan: Pinnawala in the morning, Sigiriya in the evening.

Overnight Stay: Nature resort in Dambulla/Sigiriya

Day 3: Cultural Village Tour / Pidurangala Rock

Experience a traditional village tour by tuk-tuk and boat, and learn Sri Lankan cooking at a local farmer's house. Optionally, hike Pidurangala Rock for a sunset view.

Day Plan: Cultural village tour, optional Pidurangala hike.

Overnight Stay: Resort in Sigiriya/Dambulla

Days 4-5: Relax on Pasikudah Beach

Head to the beautiful eastern coast of Sri Lanka to relax on Pasikudah beach. Enjoy optional water activities or Ayurvedic treatments.

Day Plan: Beach relaxation, optional water sports.

Overnight Stay: Resort in Pasikudah

Day 6: Polonnaruwa Ancient City / Minneriya Safari

Explore the ancient ruins of Polonnaruwa by bicycle, then enjoy an elephant safari at Minneriya National Park in the afternoon.

Day Plan: Morning cycling tour in Polonnaruwa, afternoon Minneriya safari.

Overnight Stay: Nature resort in Dambulla

Day 7: Kandy / Temple of the Tooth

Travel to Kandy and visit the Dambulla Golden Temple en route. In Kandy, see the sacred Temple of the Tooth and enjoy a panoramic view of the city.

Day Plan: Dambulla visit, Temple of the Tooth in the evening.

Overnight Stay: Mountain view hotel in Kandy
